RAJASTHAN

The Land of Forts and Palaces

Rajasthan, the Land of Kings, is a vibrant tapestry of history, culture, and desert charm. From the golden dunes of the Thar to majestic forts rising above ancient cities, every corner tells a royal tale. Its colorful festivals, traditional music, bustling bazaars, and grand palaces reflect a rich heritage that still lives on. Whether it's the pink hues of Jaipur, the blue lanes of Jodhpur, or the serene lakes of Udaipur, Rajasthan captures the soul of India in its most regal form.

Historic Cities & Highlights

  1. Jaipur - The Pink City, known for its stunning palaces like Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort.
  2. Udaipur - The City of Lakes, famous for its romantic lakes and the City Palace.
  3. Jodhpur - The Blue City, dominated by the imposing Mehrangarh Fort.
  4. Jaisalmer - The Golden City, renowned for its sandstone architecture and desert safaris.
  5. Pushkar - A holy town with a sacred lake and vibrant camel fair.

Hill Station & Nature

  1. Mount Abu - The only hill station in Rajasthan, known for its cool climate and the Dilwara Temples.
  2. Ranthambore National Park - A premier wildlife destination, famous for tiger sightings.

Cultural Experiences

  1. Chokhi Dhani (Jaipur)
  2. Camel desert camps (Jaisalmer)
  3. Folk dance & Kalbelia shows
