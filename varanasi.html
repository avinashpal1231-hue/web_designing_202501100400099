VARANASI-CITY OF MOKSHA

Varanasi, also known as Benares or Kashi, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and holds immense religious significance in India. Located on the banks of the sacred Ganges River in the state of Uttar Pradesh, Varanasi is considered a major spiritual hub for Hindus, Buddhists, and Jains.

Varanasi is renowned for its ghats, which are a series of steps leading down to the river. These ghats are used for various religious rituals, including bathing in the holy waters of the Ganges, performing cremation ceremonies, and conducting prayer ceremonies known as "aarti." The ghats are particularly vibrant during festivals and special occasions when devotees gather to celebrate and seek blessings.

Places To Visit:

Famous Temples

  1. Kashi Vishwanath Temple - The most iconic Shiva temple.
  2. Annapurna Devi Temple - Near Vishwanath Temple, goddess of nourishment.
  3. Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple - Very popular and peaceful.
  4. Durga Kund Temple - Known for its red-painted structure.

Ghats to Explore

  1. Dashashwamedh Ghat - Most famous and busiest ghat.
  2. Manikarnika Ghat - Main cremation ghat, spiritually significant.
  3. Assi Ghat - Popular for morning yoga and boat rides.
  4. Harishchandra Ghat - Another cremation ghat with historical significance.
  5. Scindia Ghat - Known for its partially submerged temple.

Experiences on The Ganga

  1. Boat ride at sunrise (must-do!)
  2. Ganga Aarti in the evening at Dashashwamedh or Assi Ghat

Historical & Cultural Spots

  1. Sarnath - Where Buddha gave his first sermon.
  2. Ramnagar Fort - Historic fort with a museum.
  3. Banaras Hindu University (BHU) - Prestigious university with a beautiful campus.
  4. Alamgir Mosque - Built by Aurangzeb, located near Manikarnika Ghat.
  5. Chunar Fort - Located about 40 km from Varanasi, offers panoramic views of the Ganges.

Local Markets

  1. Thatheri Bazaar - Famous for brassware and metal crafts.
  2. Godowlia Market - Central market for souvenirs, clothes, and street food
  3. Vishwanath Gali - Souvenirs, prasad, handicrafts.

Foods TO Try

  1. Banarasi Paan - A must-try after meals.
  2. Kachori Sabzi - Popular breakfast item.
  3. Chaat - Various street food options like Aloo Tikki, Pani Puri.
  4. Malaiyo - A seasonal winter dessert made from milk froth.
  5. Thandai - A refreshing drink, especially during festivals like Holi.
